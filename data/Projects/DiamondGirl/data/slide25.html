    Product Lightbox:
    • Scenario: Products full details view:
      When: User clicks full details view of a given product
      Then: light-box pops up with detailed description of the product with :
      - name of the product in view
      - images accordeon of the products with option to download the picture(s)
      - description text
      - the video of the product with option to download the video
      - the star-rating of the product [the rating will be set manually through the Admin panel]
      - price of the product
      - copy to clipboard option for text description copying
      - social networks icons for sharing of the product, sharing to one's account
      QUESTION: is it possible to share content to WhatsApp, instagram, twitter QUESTION: is it possible to copy images to clipboard?
