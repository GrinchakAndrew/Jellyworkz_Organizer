    Dashboard: Pending Leads:
    • General Description :
      Component : Pending Leads are the leads that no Agent yet processed \ handled, which have recently been inputted into the system. When Lead is first created, its default status is 'Pending'
      On the basis of this data the reports are created: number of sold leads today, how many pending leads today, lost leads today, how much money earned for commission from sold leads, sold leads direct earnings.
